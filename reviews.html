This review is somewhat overdue by about four months. Because my husband and I have accumulated so many items over the years, we needed a storage unit for leasing. My biggest concern was how secure was the place? I kept hearing things about units being broken into and items being stolen from other storage businesses. I can rest assured that such is not the case with Alameda Point Storage. They are pretty secure. Access to the storage area is via a password on the keypad and only up to 5:00 PM.
Even though this business is within walking distance from our home and very convenient, I am not a donkey (or an ass) carrying the load on my back to get here to unload things into the storage unit. Their monthly leasing fee is reasonable. We took advantage of their offer of leasing for three months and getting one month free. That offer has expired, but we're still leasing from them.
- Dwight Shrute
